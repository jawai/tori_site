About Me

I'm Tori! I currently work in Graphic Design at The Shopping Channel. I have a total love for all things Photoshop and have working with it for years. Recently I completed a course though HackerYou in front end development and have been diving down the rabbit hole of all things web design and I cannot get enough. In my spare time I play roller derby for the GTA Rollergirls and want to play with all the cats!
